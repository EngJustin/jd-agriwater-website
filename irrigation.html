Welcome to Irrigation and Water Engineering

Deliverables in the design of irrigation systems and or water systems.

1. DESIGN PLANS:
    detailed plans outlining the layout of the irrigation system,
    including pipe routes, valve locations and sprinkler or
    drip emitter placement.

2. HYDRAULIC CALCULATIONS:
    calculations determining water flow rates, pressure requirements,
    and pipe sizing to ensure proper distribution of water throughout
    the system.
3. EQUIPMENT SPECIFICATIONS:
    recommendations for irrigation equipment such as pumps, pipes
    valves and controllers, tailored to the specific needs of the
  sites and crops.
4. COST ESTIMATES:
    Estimates of the cost associated with purchasing and installing
    the irrigation systems, including materials, labor and any
    necessary permits.
5. OPERATIONAL AND MAINTENANCE GUIDELINES:
    instructions for operating and maintaining the irrigation
    systems to ensure optimal performance and longevity
6. WATER MANAGEMENT PLAN:
    Strategies for efficient water use, including scheduling irrigation
    events, monitoring soil moisture levels, and implimenting water saving
    technologies.
